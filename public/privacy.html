Privacy Policy

TCC Sentinel is an internal application health monitoring tool operated by The Children's Center, Inc. ("TCC"). This privacy policy describes how we handle data in connection with the Sentinel monitoring service and its associated SMS notifications.

Who this applies to

TCC Sentinel is used exclusively by TCC's business owner and authorized internal staff. It is not a consumer-facing service. No members of the public opt into or receive messages from this system.

What data we collect

Sentinel collects and stores:

  • HTTP response codes and timing from TCC's own web applications
  • Timestamps of monitoring checks
  • Alert dispatch records (what was sent to whom, when)
  • The business owner's phone number, configured by the owner, for the sole purpose of receiving operational alerts

No customer, client, employee, or third-party personal data is collected, processed, or stored by Sentinel.

How we use data

Data is used solely to notify the business owner when an internal TCC application is unavailable or malfunctioning. Data is not used for marketing, sold, shared, or provided to third parties.

SMS notifications

SMS alerts are sent exclusively to the business owner's personal phone number, which she has configured herself. No third party receives messages from this system. Standard message and data rates from the recipient's carrier may apply.

Opt-out

The sole recipient may reply STOP to any message to stop all further SMS alerts. Reply HELP for help. Help inquiries may also be directed to billing@childrenscenterinc.com.

Data retention

Monitoring check history is retained in the system's database for operational troubleshooting. The business owner may delete records at any time.

Third-party services

Sentinel relies on Twilio to deliver SMS messages and SendGrid to deliver email. Each service's own privacy policy applies to data transmitted through it.

Contact
